Who I Am

Research that sits at the intersection of users, product, and business.

I'm a UX Researcher and Designer with 10+ years of experience turning complex user behavior into clear product strategy. For five years I led research at FanDuel — one of the world's largest sports betting platforms — partnering directly with product, design, and executive teams on decisions that affected millions of users.

My work spans industries: e-commerce, healthcare, entertainment, fitness, fintech. What stays constant is my approach — grounding every recommendation in observable behavior, and making sure every insight is impossible to ignore.

"What I love about UX is the freedom to create powerful, engaging solutions — and knowing that real people are using them every day."

— Margarita

How I Think

Beyond the screen. Beyond the session.

🔍

Observation over Opinion

I design sessions that surface behavior, not just feedback. What users do tells a different story than what they say — I capture both and know which one to trust.

🧩

Insight, Not Just Findings

A finding is "users struggled with step 3." An insight is why — and what that means for the business. I never hand a team a list of problems without a frame for solving them.

📣

Research That Moves Teams

Every deliverable I produce is designed to drive a decision — not sit in a folder. I match recommendations to the right stakeholder so accountability is built in from the start.
